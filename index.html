About Golden Sip

Precision, Craft & Technology

Golden Sip was born from a vision: to create a coffee space where artisanal craftsmanship meets modern innovation. We believe your perfect cup deserves both the skill of a master barista and the precision of the latest technology.

Our atmosphere is deliberately designed — dark, minimal, and intentional — because great coffee demands your full attention. No distractions. Just the ritual.

Smart Brewing AI-assisted temperature and pressure control
Single Origin Beans sourced directly from farms worldwide
Tech Space High-speed WiFi and workstation seating
Gold Standard Award-winning roasting techniques
